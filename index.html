About The Edgewater Group

The Government depends on the private sector for top-quality products, services, and solutions to assist serving their critical missions. The Edgewater Group helps the private sector effectively and efficiently deliver their solutions to Federal agencies via the government procurement process. Our primary objective is to make you and your organization successful as you partner with and serve on the Government’s most critical missions.

Advisory Services

We assess the acquisition landscape in your target markets and set the stage for advancement based on your corporate strategic goals. We serve as a conduit between Government and Industry to support mission success through a variety of services. We can help you navigate the maze of Federal acquisition process and gain a competitive edge. Learn more

Capture Services

We lead or support strategic pursuit of a single program, bringing proven practices that enhance the probability of winning and meeting mission goals. From Campaigns (18-24 months out) to Capture Management (6 to 24 months out) to Discrete Capture Support Services (3 months out), we help execute your strategic growth plans. Learn More

Proposal Services

Government Insight

Due to our leadership roles within government, we are also able to offer programatic insights and acquisition expertise across 50 other Federal civilian agencies including the Department of Defense.
